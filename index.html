Our Story

A Cozy Corner
in the Heart of Vientiane

Golden Tree was born from a simple belief: great food should bring people together. Nestled in Phonsinuan Village, our restaurant offers a warm escape from the city — where the aroma of lemongrass and freshly brewed coffee fills the air.

We serve Asian fusion cuisine rooted in local Lao traditions, elevated with international techniques. Every ingredient is sourced fresh from local markets, every recipe crafted with care — because you deserve more than just a meal.
